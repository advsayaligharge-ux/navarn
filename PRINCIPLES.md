# NAVARN — PERMANENT PRINCIPLES (FROZEN)

The immutable core. Strategy, visuals and code may evolve within these; these do not change.

---

## 1. Brand Identity
NAVARN is **a Premium Heritage Storytelling Menswear House.**
It is **NOT** streetwear, a graphic-tee brand, a hype-culture brand, or an art-print brand.

## 2. Production Philosophy
**Launch-stage manufacturing (a decision, not an identity):** premium DTF printing · heat-press application · premium oversized cotton.
**Future production may include:** embroidery · puff · foil · mixed media · luxury embellishments · artisan collaborations.
> **Permanent rule:** *Production technique changes. NAVARN identity never changes.*

## 3. Visual DNA (LOCKED — see `lib/visual/visualDNA.ts`)
Permanent recognition signals:
premium oversized silhouette · one emotional hero statement · strong narrative continuation · large negative space · luxury composition · **gold as the luxury constant** · premium typography hierarchy · signature sleeve & edge details · premium finishing details · **story before fashion** · **emotion before decoration** · **heritage transformed into identity**.

## 4. Composition Rules
The launch established **front hero + back story** — but that is **not mandatory forever.**
> **Permanent rule — every artifact must contain:** a primary emotional statement · a secondary narrative layer · premium visual balance · luxury storytelling hierarchy.
Composition may evolve; these four requirements do not.

## 5. Color System (two approved modes)
- **Mode A — Monochromatic Luxury:** black · espresso · gold.
- **Mode B — Heritage Luxury:** ivory · indigo · heritage palette · gold accents.
Gold is present in both.

## 6. Artifact Philosophy
**Everything is an ARTIFACT.** An artifact may represent heritage, animals, birds, kings, warriors, mythology, deities, patterns, states, cities, countries, civilizations, nature, abstract concepts, or future ideas.
**Nothing is hardcoded.** India is the launch market; the universe is unlimited.
